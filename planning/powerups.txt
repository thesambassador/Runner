Powerups:
Buyable:
	Shield (absorb x hits from enemies/fireballs)
	Defensive boost (Launches player forward at high speed when you contact the "death wall")
	Double-jump (Grants double-jump ability for the run)
	Float (like Princess from Mario 2, hold jump in mid-air to float for a short duration)
	Forward respawn (skip the obstacle that you die at)
	Slide control (still be able to move left/right while sliding)
	
Unbuyable (find in level)
	Double coins for duration
	Free boost next game
	Single shield
	Invulnerability for duration
